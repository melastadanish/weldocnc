## Section 11: FAQ
**Design:** Accordion. White background. First item open.
**Heading:** CNC Machining for Los Angeles Engineers — FAQs

**Q1: How long does delivery take from China to Los Angeles?**
Total lead time to Los Angeles is 6–12 business days — 1–5 business days manufacturing in Dongguan plus 5–7 business days shipping via DHL Express or FedEx International Priority. All shipments are DDP — import duties and US customs clearance are handled and included in the quoted price. Parts arrive at your Los Angeles address with no additional import charges or broker fees.

**Q2: What tolerance can Weldo CNC hold on aerospace parts for LA customers?**
±0.005 mm is the confirmed standard tolerance for CNC milling and turning. For 5-axis simultaneous machining on complex geometry, ±0.003 mm. CMM — coordinate measuring machine — verification is included on all critical-tolerance features, with every dimension reported against your drawing and the report shipped with the parts. LA aerospace corridor work typically specifies tolerances in the ±0.005–0.015 mm range; Weldo CNC covers this range in full.

**Q3: How does Weldo CNC compare to local LA aerospace machine shops?**
Local LA aerospace shops — particularly those in El Segundo, Torrance, and Hawthorne — are experienced and often hold AS9100D certification, making them essential for programmes that require a locally certified supplier. Queue times at these shops frequently run 4–8 weeks on complex programme work. Weldo CNC is typically the stronger choice for pre-production quantities, secondary supply chain sourcing, or tight-tolerance work where the 6–12 day total delivery time is acceptable. Upload the drawing and compare quotes directly.

**Q4: Do you supply the LA aerospace and defence supply chain?**
Yes — structural brackets, actuator components, avionics housings, and precision turned aerospace hardware are a significant part of the workload for LA-region customers. CMM dimensional reports, material certificates with heat numbers, and finish process records ship with every order as standard. Weldo CNC holds ISO 9001:2015 certification — if your programme specifically requires AS9100D certification at the machining source, confirm this requirement before placing an order.

**Q5: Can you machine parts for EV and automotive programmes in the LA area?**
Yes — motor housing components, battery tray structures, chassis prototypes, and powertrain hardware in aluminium alloys, stainless steel, and engineering plastics are within standard capability. DFM review — design for manufacturability — is included with every quote. Revisions are quoted within 24 hours of file upload.

**Q6: Is there a minimum order quantity?**
No minimum. One piece to production quantities. Per-unit cost is higher on single pieces due to fixed setup cost, but there is no floor on order size. LA prototype teams and startups ordering a single CNC part pay only unit cost and DDP shipping.

**Q7: What file formats does Weldo CNC accept?**
STEP is the preferred format — it carries the 3D geometry cleanly for quoting and programming. Also accepted: IGES, SolidWorks (.sldprt), PDF 2D drawings, DXF, and compressed packages (.zip, .rar). For precision features, always include a 2D drawing with tolerance callouts alongside the STEP file. Upload directly on the quote page — response within 24 hours.

**Q8: How does pricing compare to LA machine shops for production quantities?**
For a single urgent part needed same-day, a local LA shop is the right call. From 20 parts upward — especially with full CMM documentation — Weldo CNC is typically competitive or lower on unit cost. LA aerospace corridor shops carry programme overhead and certification costs in their pricing. For pre-production quantities of 50–200 parts with full documentation, the cost difference is significant. Upload the STEP file and compare.

**Q9: Can Weldo CNC handle entertainment industry prop and rigging machining for LA customers?**
Yes — short runs with specific material and finish requirements are within standard capability. Aluminium, stainless steel, and engineering plastics with surface finishes from mirror polish to bead blast to anodizing. No minimum order. Quote within 24 hours. For parts requiring documented material composition — common in rigging where load-bearing hardware may face safety review — material certificates with heat numbers are included as standard.

**Q10: Can I visit the Weldo CNC facility?**
The facility is in Dongguan, China. Visits from engineers who want to audit the operation are welcome — arrange with advance notice. For most LA customers, the CMM dimensional report, material certificate, and ISO 9001:2015 certification provide sufficient confidence without travel. A video walkthrough is available on request.
