RapidDirect operates as an online platform with automated quoting across 10+ manufacturing processes. **Card 2 — CNC Specialism vs Multi-Process Platform**

**H2:** Weldo CNC vs RapidDirect — Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1: What tolerances does Weldo CNC hold?**
±0.005 mm as standard on CNC milling and turning. ±0.003 mm on 5-axis complex geometry. All critical-tolerance features are CMM-verified and reported per feature on the dimensional inspection report included with every order. RapidDirect does not publicly state a tolerance specification on their platform.

**Q2: What CNC machining services does Weldo CNC offer?**
CNC milling (3-axis to 5-axis), CNC turning, 5-axis CNC machining, precision grinding, wire EDM, and 50+ surface finishing options including anodizing, passivation, powder coating, nickel plating, and electropolishing. All in-house at a single facility. No subcontracting.

**Q3: What materials can Weldo CNC machine?**
100+ metals and engineering plastics. Metals include aluminium (6061, 7075, 2024, MIC-6), stainless steel (303, 304, 316L, 17-4PH), titanium (Grade 2, Grade 5), brass, copper, carbon steel, alloy steel, and tool steel. Plastics include PEEK, Delrin (POM), Nylon, Polycarbonate, ABS, PTFE, and Acrylic.

**Q4: What are the lead times?**
Prototype parts: 1–5 business days manufacturing. Small batch: 5–15 business days. Production runs: 2–5 weeks. Transit to UK, Europe, and North America: 3–7 days on top of manufacturing. Total lead time confirmed at quote — not estimated after order placement.

**Q5: Is there a minimum order quantity?**
No minimum. One part to production quantities — the same quality process applies at any volume.

**Q6: What file formats does Weldo CNC accept?**
STEP is preferred. Also accepted: IGES, SolidWorks (.sldprt), PDF 2D drawings, DXF, and compressed packages (.zip, .rar). Include a 2D drawing with tolerance callouts alongside the STEP file for any precision features.

**Q7: How does Weldo CNC pricing compare to RapidDirect?**
Both are China-based manufacturers so the cost base is comparable. The difference at volume: Weldo CNC quotes are engineer-reviewed with a clear manufacturing plan — no platform margin applied per order. For tight-tolerance work and quantities of 10–500 parts, get a direct quote and compare. Upload your STEP file and we will return a quote in 24 hours.

**Q8: Both are based in China — what is the actual difference?**
Specialism and process. RapidDirect covers 10+ manufacturing processes on an automated platform. Weldo CNC does one thing: CNC machining — 100+ dedicated machines, 120+ technicians, a QMS built specifically for machined parts. Every Weldo CNC quote is reviewed by an engineer. Every order ships with CMM report and material certificate as a default, not an option.

**Q9: Does Weldo CNC offer engineer DFM review — or is it automated like RapidDirect?**
Engineer review only — not automated. Your STEP file and drawing go to an engineer who checks tolerance feasibility, fixturing constraints, wall thickness risks, and finish interactions before the quote is returned. DFM review is included with every quote at no additional charge.

**Q10: How does international delivery work — do I need a customs broker?**
No. Weldo CNC ships DDP — Delivered Duty Paid — to the UK, USA, Germany, France, and most of Europe and North America. DDP means Weldo CNC files the customs declaration, pays the import duty, and handles clearance before parts reach the destination. Parts arrive at your address with no additional customs administration required.
