## Section 13: Quality & Certifications
**Design:** RS-01. Dark navy background. White text. H2 + 3-step checklist.
**H2:** Quality Control & Production Inspection

Three fixed steps — adapt body copy to service context:

**Step 1 — Pre-Production:**
Heading: Material [and Setup] Verified Before Cutting Starts
Body: Mill cert checked — alloy grade, heat number, temper logged. Drawing reviewed — tolerance callouts, surface finish requirements, datum references confirmed before first cut.

**Step 2 — In-Production:**
Heading: In-Process Checks at Every Setup Change
Body: Calibrated hand tools and CMM at defined intervals. Datum references re-verified after repositioning. Non-conformances trigger NCR — Non-Conformance Report — before job continues.

**Step 3 — Final Inspection:**
Heading: CMM Report Ships With Every Tight-Tolerance Order
Body: Measured values per feature — nominal, tolerance, actual, pass/fail — against your drawing revision. Dimensional report, material cert, packing list in every shipment.
