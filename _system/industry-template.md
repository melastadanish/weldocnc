## Section 9: Documentation
**Design:** 4-column icon+description grid. White background.
**Heading:** Documentation Standard for [Industry] Orders

Four fixed blocks — adapt the body copy to industry context, do not remove any block:

**Material Certificate**
Mill cert coverage: alloy grade, temper, heat number, chemical + mechanical properties, spec compliance reference (AMS/ASTM/EN). Logged before first cut. Part-to-cert traceability for multi-part orders.

**CMM Dimensional Report**
Feature-by-feature: nominal, tolerance, actual, pass/fail. Dimensional and geometric callouts (true position, perpendicularity, flatness, concentricity, profile) reported to drawing revision. Ships with parts.

**Finish Process Record**
For any post-process (anodize, passivation, nickel, chromate, etc.): process parameters, bath verification date, coating thickness, appearance check. Masking plan confirmed before processing where coating affects mating dimensions.

**First Article Inspection (FAI)**
For new parts or where required: ballooned drawing + full dimensional report + material cert + finish record. Confirm FAI requirement at quote stage.

## Section 10: Quality & Certifications
**Design:** RS-01. Dark navy background. White text.

Three fixed sub-sections — adapt body to industry context:

**Pre-Production:**
Material verified against mill cert. Drawing reviewed: GD&T callouts, finish requirements, masking plan. FAI/PPAP requirements confirmed before job enters queue.

**In-Production:**
In-process CMM at defined intervals. Datum references verified after each setup change. NCRs raised on non-conformances — out-of-tolerance parts do not proceed.

**Final-Production:**
CMM inspection against drawing revision. Nominal/tolerance/actual/pass-fail per feature. Material cert + dimensional report + finish process record + packing list ship with every order. Nothing leaves without sign-off.
